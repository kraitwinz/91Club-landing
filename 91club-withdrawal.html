❓ Troubleshooting FAQ

Money debited but not credited?

Most stuck payouts auto-revert within 30 mins. If not, contact support with Txn ID.

“KYC pending” error?

Upload clear Aadhaar front–back + PAN. Approval takes 2–4 hrs.

UPI handle not accepted?

Use mainstream handles like @oksbi, @okaxis, @paytm; corporate handles sometimes get rejected.
